Welcome to my project, a fully functional medical center management system built using Java. This web application is designed to provide an efficient and user-friendly platform where patients, doctors, and admins can seamlessly collaborate. Patients can easily book appointments, view medical histories, and access their health records. Doctors can manage their schedules, update patient records, and communicate with patients. Admins can oversee the entire system, manage users, and ensure smooth operations across the center. The system is tailored to improve the quality of healthcare services while ensuring a smooth and efficient workflow.
